About us

Built for how African education actually runs

Zivas is an academic operations platform for tertiary and secondary institutions, and for the independent educators who often teach without any institutional support at all. We automate the administrative weight of teaching — dropping assignments on schedule, grading with AI assistance, taking attendance by QR code, and generating the reports institutions already need — so an educator's time goes toward the material, not the paperwork around it.

Our vision

A continent where every learner's progress is tracked fairly, every educator is freed from repetitive admin work, and quality teaching can scale as fast as enrollment does.

Our mission

To automate the busywork of teaching — scheduling, grading, attendance, and reporting — so that educators, from a single independent tutor to a full university faculty, can focus entirely on their students.

Product

Everything an academic term needs, running in the background

One platform, built to handle the operational side of a semester end to end.

Assignment automation

Lecturers plan a semester's assignments once. Zivas drops each one on schedule and notifies students automatically.

AI-assisted grading

Hand a submission to the AI grader for a draft score and feedback — the lecturer always reviews and confirms before it's published.

QR attendance

Open a session, students scan in. An OTP fallback covers anyone whose camera or connection fails.

Learning resources

Lecturers drop readings, slides, and videos by week. Students get notified and can preview or download instantly.

Academic integrity

Every submission is screened for plagiarism and AI-generated content, with lecturers making the final call.

Analytics & reports

Continuous assessment, engagement, and weekly reports — generated automatically, not assembled by hand.
